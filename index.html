Fight your way to the end, but beware the Donsols.

Donsol is a dungeon crawler card game played with a standard 54 card deck. It was designed by John Eternal during the 2015 train jam, and turned into a mobile and desktop game later by HundredRabbits.

"Its just a strange world, and everything is strange in it. Youre making your way through an Escher drawing, collecting what you need on a path that fights back."

Zack Kotzer (Kill screen)
