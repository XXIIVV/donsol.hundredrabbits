Description

Donsol is a card game about exploring a dungeon made of a standard 54 poker card set. It was designed by John Eternal during the 2015 train jam, and turned into a mobile and desktop game later by HundredRabbits.

Developer: Hundredrabbits, based somewhere in the pacific ocean. Release date: February 2015 Stores: Itch.io Platforms: PC, Mac and Linux Price: USD $2.00 Demo: Request a demo copy.
